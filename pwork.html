AF1 Racing

AF1 Racing is a local Austin motorcycle dealer with an international presence. As the Graphic Designer, I handled the design, branding, and social media interaction for Austin our Austin location.

I created a new logo inspired by classic racing number plates to better match our Austin customer base. The new logo needed to be easier to place for social media, images, and advertisements.

Redesigning the AF1 Racing Austin website was a priority for me upon starting. I wanted the site to echo AF1's standing as one of the top dealers in the country — showing the strengths, character and lifestyle of the brand.

Along with the site redesign, I switched our e-blast client to MailChimp and created templates for both local and online customer mailers.

During my time with AF1, I got the chance to design the website and related media for a scooter rally we sponsored called the Dirty Rally. The rally project was enjoyable and a step away from the normal motorcycle shop work. I also enjoyed all the love from the rally-goers.
