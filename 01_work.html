Zephyr

Zephyr is a conceptual native-app designed to give bicycle commuters routes designed with safety and effort in mind. This project encompassed a wide range of UX and UI discovery, planning, and design.

See full overview.

SkillsUp

SkillsUp is the working title for a hackothon project. The premise is a site that would allow musicians to find other musicians to play with, either for short notice gigs, or repeating jam sessions. The time frame was 3.5 days and I was the sole designer working with one back-end, and two front-end developers.
